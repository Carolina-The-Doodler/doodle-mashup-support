Privacy Policy

Effective: September 17, 2025

Summary

  • No third-party analytics SDKs. No third-party ads.
  • No tracking, no IDFA, no fingerprinting.
  • External links and any commerce are protected by a parental gate.

Data We Process

First-party telemetry for reliability only: event name, timestamp, app version, iOS version, device model (non-unique), and coarse locale. We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.

Support Communications

If you email support@lovingartandcompany.com, we process your email address and message to respond. Email is handled through Google Workspace.

Processors

  • Google Workspace (email). Squarespace Domains (DNS). Hosting provider for API/DB (processor only).

Retention

  • Support emails: kept up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Telemetry: kept up to 30 days, then deleted or anonymized.

Children’s Privacy

The app is in Apple’s Kids Category and is designed to comply with COPPA. All external links and any commerce are behind a parental gate that cannot be disabled.

Your Rights

To request deletion of support emails or ask questions, contact support@lovingartandcompany.com.
